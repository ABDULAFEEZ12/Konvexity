Dr. Tioluwalogo Olakunbi-Black

PhD. Founder, TechTrybe Africa.

Dr. Olakunbi-Black is a performance architect, organizational systems designer, behavioral strategist, and executive coach. He is the founder of TechTrybe Africa and the driving force behind Konvexity.

His work sits at the intersection of leadership development, organizational design, and behavioral psychology. Over his career, he has helped founders, executives, and institutions across Africa build the leadership capability, operational systems, and organizational cultures required for sustained excellence.

Dr. Olakunbi-Black's approach is grounded in a fundamental conviction: that Africa's developmental challenges are not primarily resource constraints but leadership and systems constraints. The organizations that will transform the continent are those that invest intentionally in the quality of their leadership and the integrity of their organizational architecture.
