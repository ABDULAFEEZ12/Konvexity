Organizational Transformation

ADI Performance Model™

Diagnostics, accountability, and culture transformation at enterprise scale.

The ADI Performance Model is Konvexity's signature organizational transformation system. ADI stands for Assess, Design, Implement — the three phases of our proven methodology for turning underperforming organizations into structured, scalable institutions. It is not a consulting engagement. It is a complete transformation architecture.

Our approach operates across three organizational tiers. The Enterprise Tier addresses organization-wide transformation — diagnostics, accountability systems, workflow optimization, communication redesign, and culture shift. The Team Tier focuses on departmental and cross-functional alignment. The Individual Tier provides executive coaching and leadership development for the people who must carry the transformation forward. Each engagement begins with rigorous diagnostics, followed by custom system design, and culminates in supported implementation with measurable outcomes.
